- A finance tool should be created for easy tuning: bills (amount, frequency, etc.), generally bills are proportional to the total amount of assets in the condo, rent is proportional to the size and type (basement vs townhouse vs house vs condo vs penthouse). This tool will allow us to set the math about bills, including when it is considered "overdue" (impacting the credit score) and when it is too late: the repo man comes OR game over (see next lines).
- When rent cannot be paid, we can be for some time (tunable) in the negative. However after a set time, we have a repo guy coming home to seize assets. No mesh, no character, just a message that says that some our stuff was sold to repay our debt. There should be a logice depending on how important the stuff is for survival. If debt cannot be paid: Game over.
- We may introduce a credit score, as an immigrant we will start low, we can check our credit score through the smartphone. The credit score is impacted by debt, and how on time we pay bills.
- Some jobs and future condos / apt / house to rent will require a certain credit score, this is why it is important. Maybe also, the higher the credit score, the longer we can stay in debt (however it makes the credit score go down over time).
- Lastly, I want to introduce a small modification for how the skills grow: right now it appears to be linear, however it should not be. It should be more difficult to go from 90 to 100, for example, than 10 to 40.
